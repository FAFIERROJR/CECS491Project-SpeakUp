Speak Up

A cross-platform chat application for asking questions without lifting a hand.

Discussion promotes discovery and retention

Want your students to have better retention and improved test performance? Start a conversation with them. Discussions give students opportuinities to discover.

  • Why?

Extroverts dominate class disscussion

It is well known that some students will be more outspoken than others but educators have a duty to both the shy and the bold. We want to help you draw out the best in students of all personality types, not just one!

  • Why?

We should spark enduring conversations

We want students to grow intellectually by engaging with their acadmics even outside the classroom bounds

  • How?

What we do

We are creating learning spaces where any student can feel free to participate. speakup empowers teachers to use dialog in the classroom.

Instant Feedback

speakup allows teachers to lead their class in discussion and get instant feedback from students

Anonymity

Identity of students are kept hidden from other students to encourage everyone to participate

Instructor Moderated

We keep teachers in control of their discussion boards allowing them to monitor participation and stop disruptions

Child Safe

A basic profanity filter keeps conversations civil and dissuades innapropriate behavior such as bullying

Anti Spam

Keep discussion boards from becoming a fire-hydrant with anti spam filtering

Cross Platform

Cut costs by allowing students to use the hardware they already own, access speakup from Android, iOS or any modern web browser
